# Claire Vo

Serial CPO across Color, Optimizely, and LaunchDarkly, two-time founder, and creator of ChatPRD (one of the most popular AI PM tools). Known for combining product, engineering, and design under a single CPTO leader and for setting aggressive organizational pace while maintaining a high quality bar.

## Key Frameworks

### One Click Faster
Set pace expectations one iteration faster than the team's natural speed: if something feels like it needs a year, target a half. If a half, a quarter. This forces different thinking rather than just harder work. Crucially, pace should not degrade to the cadence of recurring meetings -- decisions should not wait for the next scheduled sync.

### Zone of Genius
Audit your calendar by categorizing activities into four buckets from "hated it" to "would spend all my time here." Focus ruthlessly on the top bucket. Ask yourself what you do that no one else in the organization can do -- that is your differentiated value, and leaning into it drives both career growth and personal sustainability.

### Career Bending
Know what you want, be explicit about it, and make it easy for your boss to get you there. Frame promotions as solving a problem for the company, not advancing your career. Propose org charts with your name on them when gaps emerge. The universe is bendable to your will -- organizations are fluid and can shift around talented, motivated individuals.

## Core Ideas

- The CPTO model (product + engineering + design under one leader) eliminates debates about what is best for product vs. engineering vs. design, replacing them with what is best for the organization.
- People often think later-stage companies hire her to teach them to operate like a big company, but she is actually hired to remind them they can operate like a startup.
- Personal SLA matters: as a leader, never be a bottleneck. Respond quickly, decide quickly, and maintain high throughput so the organization can move at full speed.
- PM skills will shift with AI, and the shift may come faster than anyone anticipates. The core skill becomes knowing how to leverage AI tools effectively rather than doing every task manually.
