# Anton Osika

Anton Osika is co-founder and CEO of Lovable, an AI-powered tool that turns text descriptions into fully working products. Lovable hit $4M ARR in 4 weeks and $10M ARR in 2 months with just 15 people, making it the fastest-growing startup in Europe.

## Key Frameworks

### Minimum Lovable Product
Not minimum viable, but minimum lovable. The company name itself encodes the philosophy: the best word for a great product is "lovable." Build a minimum lovable product, then a lovable product, then an absolutely lovable product.

### Generalist-First Hiring
In the AI era, being a generalist is much more important than it used to be. When assembling a product team, obsess about getting as many skill sets as possible for each person hired rather than deep specialists.

## Core Ideas

- People love the product - that's the primary driver of explosive growth, not marketing or growth hacking
- Designers can now ship real working web apps to clients instead of just designs, fundamentally changing the design profession
- Current AI coding tools are best at building first versions; iteration and database migrations remain the hardest unsolved problems
- The name "Lovable" came from product jargon about building "minimum lovable products" - the aspiration became the company identity
- Had to rewrite entire codebase from a scripting language to something more performant mid-hypergrowth, couldn't ship features for weeks
