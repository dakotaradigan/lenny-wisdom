# Aparna Chennapragada

Aparna Chennapragada is Chief Product Officer at Microsoft overseeing AI product strategy for productivity tools and agents. Previously CPO at Robinhood, VP at Google (Lens, Search, Shopping, AR, AI Assistant), and engineering leader at Akamai. Board member at eBay and Capital One.

## Key Frameworks

### NLX is the New UX
Natural language interfaces are the new user experience paradigm. Conversations have grammars, structures, and invisible UI elements. Product builders need to develop new principles and constructs for natural language as an interface rather than assuming "the model eats the product."

### Frontier Program
Institutionalize living one year in the future. Roll out cutting-edge experimental features to early adopters within enterprises without insisting the entire company change at once. Don't hold back power users while waiting for full organizational change management.

### Feature + Governance Duality
In enterprise, every feature has two jobs: making the feature work well AND governing it (security, auditability, compliance). Consumer product builders transitioning to enterprise often either ignore governance or over-cripple UX. The art is balancing both.
