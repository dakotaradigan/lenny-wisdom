## Key Frameworks

### The Aladdin and the Genie
AI tools have a context memory window (the three wishes) and humans have a clarity problem (wishing to be "taller" and becoming 13 feet). The machine limitation is tokens; the human limitation is vague instructions. Since you cannot control token limits, optimize 100% of your effort on clarity, specificity, and providing references.

### Progressive Clarity Through Parallel Builds
Start five projects in parallel to refine your idea: (1) brain dump prompt, (2) structured typed prompt, (3) screenshot from Mobbin or Dribbble as reference, (4) code snippet from a component library. By the time you compare all outputs, you have far more clarity than sitting with a blank page, and you actually save credits long-term despite higher upfront cost.

## Core Ideas

- Spend 80% of time planning and chatting with AI, only 20% executing; most people optimize for the wrong kind of speed
