# Carilu Dietrich

Former CMO who advised CEOs and CMOs at hypergrowth B2B companies including Segment, Miro, 1Password, and Sprout Social. Most notably headed marketing at Atlassian through their IPO.

## Key Frameworks

### Hypergrowth Requirements
Three essentials: 1) Organic, inbound, viral word of mouth (you can't pay enough to grow at hypergrowth rates). 2) An amazing product people love. 3) Riding the lightning by continuously hiring 2-3x leaders who've seen the next stage of growth.

### Career Acceleration Through Company Momentum
Your career trajectory depends heavily on your company's momentum. At Oracle, a team grew from 5 to 7 in five years. At Atlassian, 15 to 100 in four years. Pick great companies and your career accelerates through the network and opportunities.

## Core Ideas

- To reach the C-suite: think in terms of revenue, speak the language of CEOs and boards, build cross-functional relationships, and pick high-quality companies
- Working 2 extra hours daily for 5 years compounds into years of additional experience; there are no shortcuts to deep knowledge
- Take "tours of duty" across departments to understand how the whole system works; future C-suite leaders need systems thinking
- The quality of your first company propels your entire career through people and network effects
- Great companies attract great people who disperse and create opportunities for each other

## Notable Perspectives

> "In order to get hypergrowth, you have to have organic, inbound, and viral word of mouth. You can't pay enough to grow at those rates and have a viable company."

> "Hypergrowth companies go through stages that would take other companies five or ten years. They need to keep hiring 2X and 3X leaders who have seen the next stage of growth."
