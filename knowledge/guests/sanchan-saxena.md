# Sanchan Saxena

VP of Product at Coinbase. Previously Head of Product at Airbnb where he scaled the PM org from 30 to 200 people while navigating COVID. Founded and led Instagram Shopping. Director at Yahoo and PM at Microsoft. Has worked closely with Brian Chesky, Brian Armstrong, Zuck, and Kevin Systrom.

## Key Frameworks

### Little Bets Career Strategy
Career paths are not planned -- they emerge from taking little bets, trying things out, building rapid recovery when they fail, and iterating. Sanchan started in aerospace engineering, pivoted to a startup, pivoted to Microsoft, then stumbled into product management. The meta-lesson: take steps, learn, pivot; five-year career plans do not work.

### Three Types of PM Learning
Starter learning (getting from 0 to 1), scaler learning (taking a proven thing and growing it), and optimizer learning (refining at massive scale). Choose your next role based on which type of learning you need. Large companies like Amazon teach scaling; startups teach starting. Know what gives you energy and optimize for that.

## Core Ideas

- At Airbnb, the product is not the app -- it is the memories created in the physical space; this real-world product lens changes how you build software
- Brian Chesky's superpower: bending reality so people leave reviews believing the impossible is actually possible
- Instagram was pre-revenue when Sanchan joined (under 50 engineers); the moment Zuck said "go make revenue" launched a journey to billions in ad revenue
- The art and science of PM must be mastered together -- too scientific means missing far-fetched opportunities; too artistic means lacking rigor
