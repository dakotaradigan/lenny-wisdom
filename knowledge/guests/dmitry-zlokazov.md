# Dmitry Zlokazov

Global Head of Product at Revolut, the $60B+ finance super app operating in 50+ countries with 50M+ customers. Under his leadership, Revolut has become one of the top three companies for producing and hiring excellent product managers, alongside Palantir and Intercom.

## Key Frameworks

### Local CEO Product Owner Model
Product owners at Revolut are end-to-end responsible for their product, their business metrics, and their customers' happiness. Engineers, designers, data analysts, and operations managers all report to the product owner as line manager. Three specializations exist (UX, technical, data science) but 85-90% of the skillset is shared across all types.

### Wow Product Obsession
In fintech, lovable UX is the most neglected differentiator. Revolut invests heavily in making every flow not just functional but emotionally resonant. If something is 99% done, it is closer to 0% than 100% because the last 1% of polish is what makes users love the product. Founders review 100% of screens before they ship.

## Core Ideas

- Hire for raw intellect and unquenched hunger to build, not experience. The most successful product owners often come from internal transfers (engineers or operations managers) who already have culture fit and domain knowledge.
- When a product is not getting traction, it may not be because the underlying idea is wrong -- the product may just suck. By forcing teams to build products people love, you eliminate one entire dimension of uncertainty about product-market fit.
- Going deep means really deep: product owners regularly sit with engineers and read code. They must understand regulatory requirements at the level of specific compliance rules across 50 countries, then zoom out to build scalable frameworks.
- Weekly product reviews directly with founders (CEO and CTO) for every team create both accountability and invaluable steering. This flat hierarchy keeps the entire product org aligned on quality and direction.
