# Ada Chen Rekhi

Ada Chen Rekhi is an executive coach focused on helping founders scale themselves, and co-founder of Notejoy. She was previously SVP of Marketing at SurveyMonkey and led marketing for LinkedIn's growth team after her contact management startup was acquired by LinkedIn.

## Key Frameworks

### Curiosity Loops
A structured method for gathering contextual advice by asking specific, unbiased questions to a curated group of 5-10 people, then processing responses and closing the loop with gratitude. Fights bad advice by making feedback contextual rather than generic.

### Values Exercise
A lightweight 10-15 minute exercise where you select from a list of values words, filter them into groups, and stack-rank into 3-5 sentences. Creates an internal scorecard for decision-making that counters external pressures of status and money.

### Eating Your Vegetables
The principle that you need 10-12 exposures to something before you can distinguish genuine dislike from unfamiliarity. Applies to career skills, networking, and content creation.

## Core Ideas

- Curiosity loops are "user research for your life" - ask good questions (specific, soliciting rationale, unbiased) to people who know the subject and who know you well
- Values should serve as an internal scorecard for decisions, helping you resist obvious-seeming choices that don't align with what actually matters to you
- Coaches are not always the best solution; explore alternatives like curiosity loops, courses, and building a supportive community before hiring a coach
- Force yourself through initial discomfort with concrete rules (e.g., hand out 10 business cards, touch the back wall) to build foundational skills
