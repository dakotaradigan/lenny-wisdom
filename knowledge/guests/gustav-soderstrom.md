# Gustav Soderstrom

Co-President, Chief Product Officer, and Chief Technology Officer at Spotify. Has been at Spotify for over 14 years, starting as head of mobile and growing into responsibility for all product, design, data, and engineering. Previously founded mobile companies and sold one to Yahoo. Despite the same title, the job has been like starting a new company every 6-12 months as Spotify scaled from Nordic startup to global public company.

## Key Frameworks

### Curation to Recommendation to Generation
The internet started with user curation (digitize content, let users organize it -- Facebook, Spotify). Then it shifted to recommendation (algorithms replace human curation -- a shift that required rethinking UX and sometimes business models). Now we are entering generation, where AI creates rather than curates or recommends. Each era requires fundamental product rethinking. Do not treat generative AI as "more of the same" machine learning -- it is something completely new.

### Internal Podcasting for Culture
Spotify created an internal podcast where leadership interviews each other to make senior leaders more approachable. Listening to someone talk for an hour makes them feel real in a way town halls and docs cannot. This later became an external podcast for recruitment. People consistently cite it as a key reason for joining Spotify.

## Core Ideas

- When you stay at a fast-growing company for 14 years, you effectively start a new company every 6-12 months due to scale changes, making the experience equivalent to multiple jobs
- Generative AI products (like Spotify's AI DJ) should be measured by whether they could exist without generative AI; incremental improvements using LLMs are separate from true generative product innovation
- Creating content (a podcast) as a product leader builds empathy for creators, which is essential when your platform serves creators
- Storytelling is more effective than documents for sharing strategy and culture; people will not read long strategy docs, but they will listen to compelling stories
- Mobile product development at Spotify required both product and business model innovation because streaming could not work on edge networks and could not be funded by ads
