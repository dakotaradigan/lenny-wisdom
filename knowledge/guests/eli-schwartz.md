# Eli Schwartz

Growth advisor specializing in SEO who has helped companies like Quora, Coinbase, Tinder, LinkedIn, WordPress, and Zapier develop and execute SEO strategies. Author of Product-Led SEO. Known for his refreshing, product-minded approach to SEO and his analysis of how AI is fundamentally reshaping the search landscape.

## Key Frameworks

### Product-Led SEO
Think of SEO as a product, not a marketing tactic. Product managers should own SEO strategy because it is fundamentally a product question: what will a user search for to discover this product, and how do we position the product to serve that self-discovery journey? If you cannot answer what someone would search to find you, do not do SEO.

### The New Search Funnel (AI Overviews Era)
AI Overviews and LLM chatbots are swallowing the top-of-funnel discovery layer of search. The user journey now starts with an AI-summarized answer that provides direction, then moves to mid-funnel search where SEO matters most. The opportunity shifts from ranking on broad discovery keywords to owning the specific, intent-rich queries that come after AI gives initial direction.

## Core Ideas

- AI Overviews are not killing SEO -- they are forcing a necessary evolution. The old model of ranking #1 on broad keywords with freelancer-written content was already serving users poorly. AI answers replace that layer, and the real opportunity is in the mid-funnel where users have specific intent.
- SEO is not a dark art. Step one -- the step almost everyone misses -- is identifying what someone would actually search for to find your product. Most SEO failures start with targeting keywords that do not match real user intent.
- Google is rolling out AI Overviews much more aggressively than most realize -- they are now appearing on most search results, for non-logged-in users, and expanding internationally. This is not a test; it is the new reality.
- Payback period for SEO investments is long, but the compounding returns are enormous. Companies that invest in genuine user value through SEO build moats that are very difficult for competitors to replicate.
