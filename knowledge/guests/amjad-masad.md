# Amjad Masad

Amjad Masad is co-founder and CEO of Replit, an AI-powered software development and deployment platform with 34 million users globally. Replit enables anyone to describe an idea in natural language and get a fully working product, abstracting away the complexity of runtimes, package managers, and deployment.

## Key Frameworks

### End-to-End Software Creation
Replit differentiates by owning the entire software lifecycle - from writing code to deploying and monetizing - rather than just one piece. This makes it harder to adopt for enterprises but enables complete novices to build and ship real products.

### Ideas as the New Bottleneck
Historically, product teams were bottlenecked by engineering capacity. AI tools remove that bottleneck, making the ability to generate and evaluate ideas the scarce resource rather than the ability to build.

## Core Ideas

- An 11-year-old can now build and deploy a full working application, including database, hosting, and backend - the barrier to software creation has fundamentally shifted
- AI agents in Replit are structured as "another user" in their multiplayer system, coding alongside you in real time
- Current AI coding tools are great at building MVPs but struggle with iteration and database migrations as products evolve
- In 5 years, someone could run a billion-dollar company with zero employees where support, development, and operations are all handled by AI
- The future of AI coding tools includes the agent being able to "grab a human" when it gets stuck

## Notable Perspectives

> "The idea behind Replit is that making software today is very difficult. We want to make it easier."

> "I could imagine whatever five years from now, someone running a billion-dollar company with zero employees where the support is handled by AI, the development is handled by AI."
