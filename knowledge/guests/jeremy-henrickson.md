# Jeremy Henrickson

Product leader at Rippling. Previously Chief Product Officer at Coinbase during the 2017 crypto boom (40x usage growth in one year). Expert on maintaining product velocity through hypergrowth and uncertainty.

## Key Frameworks

### Small Teams with Clear Missions
The universal truth for maintaining velocity at scale: break large problems into sufficiently small bits that small groups can attack wholeheartedly, minimizing horizontal communication. Pair this with clear platform investments that reduce decision-making complexity for domain teams.

### Leaders in the Trenches
Resist the temptation to float above and delegate from 10,000 feet. The real learning about challenges and opportunities comes from being in the trenches on the hardest, most complicated initiative alongside your team.

## Core Ideas

- During Coinbase's 40x growth, the team maintained focus by never talking about people getting rich -- it was always about customers and their money, with security as the non-negotiable priority.
- In crypto (and any high-uncertainty domain), you must be able to have intense debates about fundamental questions, then come out with a clear company point of view that everyone executes toward at full speed -- until you decide to change direction.
- Crucible moments of extreme pressure are where the most accelerated personal growth happens. Setting those experiences in context -- recognizing what you built and learned -- helps teams endure the hardest stretches.
- The choice of who you work with matters more than almost anything else in high-growth environments. Trust and the ability to stand shoulder-to-shoulder in the trenches is essential.

## Notable Perspectives

> "It's very tempting to float up here as a leader. Where you really learn is by being there with the people in the trenches on whichever thing seems hardest."

> "Go full speed toward this answer until you decide to go full speed toward a different answer."
